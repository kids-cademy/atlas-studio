Leul este o specie din familia Felidae. Este o pisică musculoasă, cu pieptul adânc, cu capul rotunjit, gâtul scurt și urechile rotunde și un smoc de păr la capătul cozii.

Este o specie socială, formând grupuri numite familii.

The lion is an apex and keystone predator, although some lions scavenge when opportunities occur, and have been known to hunt humans, although the species typically does not.

Lions are one of the largest cats in the world.

They live in southern and eastern parts of Africa.

Lions are only social cats which live in groups called prides .

Each pride is composed of one or two males and large number of females.

Larger prides can consist of around 40 members.

Life in a group increases the chance for successful hunt and provides protection of cubs.
